The Help You Don't Need

Tinnitus Tuning

A sound professional will tune your ears to the most annoying noises in your home, moving you from blissful ignorance to hyper-awareness. We simply help you hear the problems better. You will pay us to listen to your problems, quite literally.

Procrastination Partner

A professional will sit next to you while you work, sighing periodically and asking if you've made any progress yet. They are here to silently judge you, to breathe your air, and to make you feel just a little bit worse about yourself.

Existential Plumbing

An apprentice plumber will stare into your drains and question the meaning of it all. No actual plumbing work is done, but the professional will offer unanswerable questions about the nature of water, entropy, and the futility of home maintenance.

Confrontational Pest Control

Our specialist will not eliminate your pests but instead act as a third-party mediator. They will sit down with your rodents and insects to "hear them out" and help them articulate their needs and grievances.

Personal Waxing

Our waxing specialist will arrive with a premium selection of automotive waxes and proceed to meticulously polish your skin, ensuring a deep, lustrous shine. Our professional is trained to only use products that would leave a car gleaming.

Curated Apologies

A specialist will arrive to deliver a non-specific apology on your behalf. They will not apologize for any specific action you've taken, but for something entirely unrelated, like the human condition or the state of the world.
